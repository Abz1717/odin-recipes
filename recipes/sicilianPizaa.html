Sicilian Pizza Perfection

Embark on a flavor-filled journey with our Sicilian Pizza, a culinary masterpiece that captures the essence of authentic Italian cuisine. This square-shaped delight boasts a thick, airy crust that provides the perfect canvas for a symphony of savory toppings and rich tomato sauce.

The heart of this Sicilian Pizza is its robust tomato sauce, a harmonious blend of ripe tomatoes, garlic, and a medley of Italian herbs. Each spoonful of the sauce is a burst of freshness that complements the substantial yet pillowy crust.

The toppings are a celebration of traditional flavors. Generous layers of mozzarella cheese melt to perfection, creating a gooey and indulgent texture. Slices of pepperoni, savory sausage, and earthy mushrooms add a depth of flavor, while black olives and fresh basil contribute a Mediterranean twist.

Baking our Sicilian Pizza is a transformative process. The crust, golden and crisp on the outside, gives way to a soft and airy interior. The aroma of bubbling cheese and fragrant herbs fills the air, enticing you with the promise of a truly authentic pizza experience.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough:
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
    • 1 1/2 cups warm water
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Tomato Sauce:
    • 2 cans (28 oz each) crushed tomatoes
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toppings:
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Pepperoni slices
    • Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
    • Sliced mushrooms
    • Black olives, sliced
    • Fresh basil leaves

    Steps:

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough:
    • In a bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water with sugar. Let it sit until foamy.
    • In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead until a smooth dough forms. Let it rise until doubled in size.
    • Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Make the Tomato Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until fragrant. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Assemble and Bake:
    • Press the pizza dough into a well-oiled square baking pan.
    • Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, followed by mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, olives, and fresh basil.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  4. Serve:
    • Allow the Sicilian Pizza to cool slightly before slicing. Serve and savor the authentic flavors of Sicily.
