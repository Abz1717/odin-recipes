Shawarma Delight

Indulge in the exotic flavors of our delicious Shawarma creation. This mouthwatering recipe combines layers of succulent marinated meat with a medley of vibrant spices, creating a taste sensation that will transport your taste buds to the streets of the Middle East.

The star of this culinary adventure is the perfectly seasoned meat, thinly sliced and packed with bold flavors. The marinade, a blend of garlic, yogurt, Middle Eastern spices, and a hint of lemon, infuses the meat with an irresistible aroma and taste that will leave you craving more.

Assembling our Shawarma is an art form. Thin pita bread cradles the flavorful meat, and each bite is complemented by a symphony of accompaniments. Freshly diced tomatoes, crisp lettuce, and tangy pickles add a refreshing crunch, while our signature tahini sauce provides a creamy and nutty undertone that ties the dish together.

Once assembled, our Shawarma undergoes a final transformation on the grill, achieving the perfect balance of char and tenderness. The aroma that fills the air is a testament to the culinary delight that awaits.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) thinly sliced beef or chicken
  • Marinade:
    • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
    • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • Juice of 1 lemon
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Accompaniments:
    • Thinly sliced tomatoes
    • Shredded lettuce
    • Diced pickles
    • Pita bread
    • Tahini sauce

    Steps:

  1. Marinate the Meat:
    • In a bowl, mix together minced garlic, yogurt, cumin, coriander, paprika, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
    • Add the thinly sliced meat to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well coated. Marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for enhanced flavor.
  2. Grill the Shawarma:
    • Thread the marinated meat onto skewers or cook on a grill pan over medium-high heat until fully cooked and slightly charred.
    • Remove from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing the meat into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the Shawarma:
    • Warm the pita bread and place a generous portion of the sliced meat on each piece.
    • Add sliced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, diced pickles, and a drizzle of tahini sauce.
  4. Serve:
    • Roll up the Shawarma and serve hot. Enjoy this culinary journey with a side of your favorite Middle Eastern accompaniments.
