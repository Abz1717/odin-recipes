Lasagna - Recipe #1

Back to Home

Description

In this classic lasagna recipe, layers of savory goodness come together to create a comforting and flavorful dish. The heart of the recipe lies in a robust meat sauce crafted from ground beef, onions, and garlic, simmered to perfection with crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. The addition of red wine, though optional, introduces a subtle richness.

Assembling the lasagna involves the careful layering of cooked noodles with a luscious cheese mixture. The cheese filling, a velvety combination of ricotta, egg, fresh parsley, salt, and pepper, adds a creamy and indulgent texture to each bite. This layers-on-layers approach continues, with each tier featuring a symphony of flavors – the meaty tomato sauce, the richness of mozzarella, and the sharpness of Parmesan.

Once assembled, the lasagna enters the oven to undergo a delightful transformation. Covered initially to ensure even cooking, it emerges bubbling and golden after a stint in the heat. The aroma wafting from the oven is a promise of the delectable amalgamation of ingredients within.

Allowing the lasagna to cool slightly before serving ensures that each slice retains its form, revealing the strata of flavors beneath. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with garlic bread or a fresh green salad, this homemade lasagna is a testament to the joy of classic, comforting Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

    For the meat sauce:
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

    • For the cheese Mixture:
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Additional Ingredients:
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    Steps:


  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
    • Add chopped onions and garlic to the pot and sauté until they are softened.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, red wine (if using), basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer the sauce for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a baking dish, spread a small amount of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    • Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  4. Bake:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  5. Serve:
    • Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Serve with a side of garlic bread or a green salad.
